About
Our objectives are to create and develop innovative techniques for visualization of biomedical data. These techniques are developed for application in clinically-focused, disease-specific manners. The uniqueness of bio signal processing research stems from the tight coupling between the ECE Department, the faculty of Medicine and the UBC hospital.
